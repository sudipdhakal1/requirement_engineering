## ğŸ§© Project Structure & Deliverables

The semester-long project follows the requirements engineering lifecycle:

### Phase 1: Business Analysis
- **Deliverable:** Business Requirements Specification (BRS)
- Focus on business context, stakeholders, goals, and constraints

### Phase 2: Requirements Elicitation
- **Deliverables:** Use Case Diagram, Use Case Descriptions, User Stories
- Stakeholder interviews, discovery techniques, and scenario analysis

### Phase 3: Requirements Analysis & Reconciliation
- Conflict resolution, prioritization, and feasibility analysis
- Refinement of functional and non-functional requirements

### Phase 4: Requirements Modeling & Representation
- **Deliverables:** Refined system boundary, updated use cases, supporting models
- Emphasis on clarity, consistency, and traceability

### Phase 5: Requirements Verification & Validation
- Review for correctness, completeness, and testability
- Stakeholder validation activities

### Phase 6: Requirements Management
- Change handling, versioning, and impact awareness
- Lifecycle thinking beyond initial documentation
